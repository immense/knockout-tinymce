Knockout TinyMCE Demo

§ Simple example

Use the tinymce binding on any textarea element.

            window.view1 = {
              string_of_html: ko.observable()
            };

            ko.applyBindings(view1, $('#simple-example-section .result')[0]);
          
            <textarea data-bind='tinymce: string_of_html'>
            <p>
              <strong>Value:</strong>
              <span data-bind='text: string_of_html'></span>
            </p>
